What I Built

DocMinds.ai — Clinical AI Platform
Founder & Lead Engineer | 2025 – Present

Production clinical AI platform for physicians. The doctor asks a clinical question in natural language and receives an evidence-based response with verified citations in under 2 seconds. Accessible via web and WhatsApp.

What I engineered: Hybrid RAG (BM25 + semantic + Reciprocal Rank Fusion) with zero citation hallucinations. Self-hosted medical LLM (AWQ quantized) with 4-model fallback chain achieving 80–95% cost reduction. 5-layer safety system with PII scrubbing, drug interaction checks, and regulatory disclaimers. 10 modules: clinical chat, lab interpretation (22+ analytes, deterministic classification), ECG analysis (ResNet-1D trained on 345K Brazilian ECGs), ambient clinical scribe (Whisper + speaker diarization), adaptive quiz engine (1,053+ real exam questions), spaced repetition flashcards (ts-fsrs), interactive clinical cases, scientific library, WhatsApp channel, and gamification.
